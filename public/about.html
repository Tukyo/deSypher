What is deSypher?

deSypher is an Onchain word puzzle game where you have to guess the correct five letter word within the alloted guesses.

  • Correct letter, correct position.
  • Correct letter, incorrect position.
  • Incorrect letter.

To start playing, you will need a wallet. After you have a wallet, you will need to acquire $SYPHER tokens to play.

Getting Started

If you don't have a wallet, use one of the buttons below to begin the onboarding process. Please note: you may run into issues with multiple wallets installed.

Funding Your Wallet

After you have installed a wallet, you will need to acquire $SYPHER tokens to play. You can do this by swapping for the token on Uniswap.

Disclaimer

deSypher is a game of skill. Success depends on your ability to decipher clues and use your knowledge of words to guess the correct answer within a limited number of attempts. While we provide odds of success for informational purposes, your progress and success are primarily determined by skill and strategy.

On your first guess the odds are a 1:3100 chance of guessing the correct word. These odds are based on the total number of possible 5-letter words in the puzzle database.

By playing the game, you acknowledge that you are paying to access and play a game of skill. Your payment grants you the opportunity to engage in a word puzzle challenge that tests your vocabulary, logic, and deductive reasoning.

It is important to understand that deSypher is not a gambling platform. The outcome of the game is not left to chance but is instead influenced by your skillful play. The payment you make to play the game is not a wager but a fee for access to a skill-based challenge.
